Case study

Summer Savings

Summer Savings was a seasonal rewards campaign designed to encourage Beyond members to return to the OVO app each week through exclusive offers from brands including Papa Johns, Costa and Cineworld.

Role
Product Designer
Skills
  • End-to-end journey mapping
  • Design systems
  • Reward & redemption flows
  • Content design
  • Cross-team collaboration

Overview

Summer Savings was a seasonal rewards campaign designed to encourage Beyond members to return to the OVO app each week through exclusive offers from brands including Papa Johns, Costa and Cineworld.

I helped design the end-to-end experience, from discovering rewards in the app through to redeeming them with third-party partners. Working closely with Product, Brand and Engineering, I created reusable design patterns that made the journey simple for customers and scalable for future campaigns.

The challenge

Customers needed to move between the OVO app and partner websites to claim rewards. Each partner had different requirements, making the experience feel fragmented and inconsistent.

The challenge was to create a seamless journey that customers could understand quickly while supporting multiple partners and new offers every week.

Design process

I mapped the end-to-end journey to identify opportunities to reduce friction and improve consistency.

Rather than designing each reward individually, I created reusable patterns for reward pages, redemption flows and supporting content. This gave customers a familiar experience every time they claimed an offer while helping the team deliver new campaigns more efficiently.

I also redesigned the third-party redemption experience, simplifying the language, improving guidance and creating a more consistent experience beyond the OVO app.

The same pattern, applied across partners.

Impact

The campaign generated strong engagement despite receiving no promotion during its first 10 days.

12,000+ customers engaged organically
4,000 free pizzas claimed in 21 hours
7,500 restaurant vouchers redeemed

Weekly offers regularly sold out within days

Key takeaways

This project reinforced the value of designing systems, not just individual screens. By creating reusable patterns and collaborating across teams, we delivered an experience that was easier for customers to use and quicker for the business to scale.
