Case study — OVO Beyond

Summer savings: a campaign that sold out

Leading the content for a Beyond loyalty campaign that customers claimed faster than the team could restock it.

Role
Lead content design
Tools
Figma
Skills
Brand alignment, stakeholder collaboration, third-party content review

Overview

OVO's Beyond programme ran a summer campaign mixing freebies and discounts — free pizza from Papa John's, coffee from Costa — to bring customers back to the app more often.

I led the content end-to-end: from the first in-app teaser through redemption and the follow-up email, working closely with Product, Brand, and partner teams to keep everything joined up.

Process

Discover

Reviewed how other apps frame rewards, and built on what we'd learned from the previous "Winter Wins" campaign rather than starting from a blank page.

Define

Designed the entry points — the Beyond home section, the Offers page — and wrote push notification content built to be short and action-focused.

Design

Set word-count limits and a repeatable content pattern for every reward modal — title, tagline, summary, how-to-claim steps, T&Cs, CTA — plus separate messaging for customers not yet eligible, to support acquisition.

Deliver

Rewrote the partner-hosted landing pages from scratch: clearer headlines, helpful form hints, human error messages, and consistent confirmation copy.

Key screens & decisions

Results

4,000 free pizzas claimed
21 hrs until week one's quota sold out
7,500 vouchers claimed for £5 off local restaurants
12,000 customers engaged organically in 10 days, zero promotion

Why it worked

Reusing and refining patterns from Winter Wins meant the team could move fast without relearning the basics. Repeatable content structures kept delivery quick even as new rewards were added mid-campaign, and a consistent, human tone across partner pages and push notifications built the trust that made customers actually claim what was on offer.
