About me

I specialise in turning complex problems into simple experiences. My background in content design helps me create products that are easier to understand, easier to use and grounded in customer needs. I'm currently a designer at OVO Energy.

My career hasn't followed a conventional path.

Before transitioning into product and tech, I worked in marketing. While it's an unusual start for a designer, it taught me how to communicate effectively, adapt to diverse audiences, and wear many different hats.

For the past six years, I've designed digital products across energy, telecommunications, government, and retail. I'm a true generalist, and I like it that way — I'm just as comfortable mapping out complex user journeys as I am polishing high-fidelity UI.

I thrive in environments with clear goals, highly motivated collaborators, and enough momentum to ship great work regularly.
